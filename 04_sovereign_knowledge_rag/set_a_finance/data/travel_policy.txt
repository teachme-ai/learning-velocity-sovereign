# Global Corporate Travel & Expense Policy

## 1. General Provisions
All employees are required to act responsibly and professionally when incurring and submitting expenses.

## 2. Air Travel
- Domestic flights must be economy class.
- International flights exceeding 8 hours may be booked in business class, subject to Senior VP approval.
- All international travel requires VP approval prior to booking.

## 3. Lodging
- Standard hotel room limits are $250 per night.
- In high-cost designated cities (e.g., New York, London, Tokyo), the limit is increased to $400 per night.

## 4. Meals and Entertainment
- Meal expenses cannot exceed $50 per day for domestic travel.
- Meal expenses for international travel cannot exceed $75 per day.
- Alcohol is generally not a reimbursable expense unless as part of an approved client entertainment dinner (limit 2 drinks per person).

## 5. Technology and Equipment
- IT hardware (laptops, phones) must be procured through the central IT catalog.
- Software subscriptions exceeding $50/month require IT and Manager approval.
- Cloud computing resources (AWS, GCP, Azure) require explicit sign-off from the Director of Engineering if exceeding $500/month.
