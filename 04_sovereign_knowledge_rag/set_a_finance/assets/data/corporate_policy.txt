1. OVERVIEW
This policy outines the rules for corporate expenditure. All employees must adhere to these guidelines to ensure data integrity and financial sovereignty.

2. CATEGORY THRESHOLDS
- TRAVEL: Maximum $500 per night for lodging.
- MEALS: Maximum $100 per day.
- TECHNOLOGY: All server and software expenses must be approved by the Department Head if over $10,000.
- OFFICE SUPPLIES: Maximum $5,000 per quarter per department.

3. APPROVAL HIERARCHY
- Level 1: Expenses < $1,000 (Manager approval)
- Level 2: Expenses $1,000 - $10,000 (Director approval)
- Level 3: Expenses > $10,000 (VP/SVP approval)

4. AUDIT PROCEDURES
All expenses are subject to forensic analysis. Suspicious patterns, such as multiple transactions just below a threshold, will be flagged for investigation by the Sovereign Audit Committee.

5. NON-COMPLIANCE
Failure to comply with these rules will result in an immediate freeze of the expense account and a formal review.
