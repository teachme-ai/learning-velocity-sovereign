**Introduction**
===============

The Sustainable and Responsible Investment (ESG) industry is a rapidly evolving field that requires professionals to analyze and understand the environmental, social, and governance (ESG) factors influencing companies' financial performance. In this lab manual, you will learn how to design a comprehensive Sustainability & ESG data-driven project using sovereign reasoning tools.

**Goal**
--------

In this lab, your objective is to develop a Project Brief and Audio Overview for a Supply Chain-related sustainability issue by leveraging the **Base Track: Narrative Engine (No-Code)** in NotebookLM. This will enable you to autonomously analyze and summarize the relevant supply chain data from Session 01, providing actionable insights into responsible business practices.

**Step-by-Step Guide**
-------------------------

### Step 1: Set up NotebookLM

*   Open **NotebookLM** and create a notebook titled "Supply Chain Industry Brief".

### Step 2: Add Source

*   Upload your cleaned structured CSV/Sheets data to **NotebookLM**. This will be used as the foundation for our analysis.

### Step 3: Generate Project Brief & Podcast

*   Click "Project Brief" in the Notebook Guide.
*   Open the Audio Overview panel and click "Generate". This will help us summarize key findings from your sourced data.
