# Introduction
This technical curriculum is designed to educate and guide participants in implementing sustainability-focused expense policies within their organizations. These guidelines are rooted in industry standards and best practices, ensuring compliance while promoting environmentally responsible business practices.

## Business Value
The implementation of these guidelines will have several benefits for your organization:

* **Cost Savings**: Reducing travel and lodging expenses can lead to significant financial savings, which can be allocated towards sustainable initiatives or used to offset the cost of implementing new ESG measures.
* **Enhanced Credibility**: By adopting local-first policies that prioritize sustainability over traditional business practices, you will enhance your organization's reputation as a responsible corporate citizen.
* **Improved Employee Engagement**: Transparency around sustainable expense policies and transparent decision-making processes can increase employee trust and engagement in the company's mission to reduce its environmental footprint.
* **Access to ESG Research and Tools**: By staying up-to-date with industry best practices, you will be able to utilize relevant research and tools, providing valuable insights for your organization's sustainability strategies.

These guidelines are tailored to support a range of organizations operating in various industries, from finance and technology to manufacturing and non-profit sectors. By following these principles, you can help minimize the environmental impact of your business while maximizing its social responsibility.
