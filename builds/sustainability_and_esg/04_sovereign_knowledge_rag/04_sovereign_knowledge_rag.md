**Introduction**
### The Objective
Modern AI models lack contextual understanding of internal company documents, making it challenging to retrieve relevant information from these sources. In this lab, we aim to build a **Sovereign Knowledge Base (SKB)** using Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG). Our goal is to construct a pipeline that ingests corporate travel policies, generates local mathematical representations using Ollama, and securely stores them in a local FAISS vector database for querying without sending data to the cloud.

### Persona
As a technical curriculum expert, we will focus on creating an enterprise-focused environment, providing empathetic support throughout the lab. Our approach is designed to empower businesses to leverage modern AI models while ensuring they are aware of their internal company documents and their context.

**Business Value**

This RAG pipeline has numerous benefits for organizations:

*   **Improved Decision-Making**: By leveraging SKBs, companies can make more informed decisions by accessing relevant data from internal sources.
*   **Enhanced Operational Efficiency**: Automated queries reduce the need for manual data retrieval, freeing up resources for more strategic tasks.
*   **Better Integration with AI Models**: The local vector database enables seamless integration of AI models that require contextual understanding, ensuring a more effective and accurate outcome.
*   **Increased Transparency and Trust**: By storing policy documents and query results locally, organizations can demonstrate their commitment to transparency and trustworthiness.

By successfully building and deploying this RAG pipeline, businesses can unlock new possibilities for sustainability and ESG-related decision-making, while maintaining control over sensitive information.
